CHALLENGE

CRUX Building x Restoration needed a full branding experience. First a logo was created. Next business cards. Lastly, and most importantly, CRUX was lacking any web presence so a website was necessary. Naturally, priority number one became creating a mobile-ready website to attract and acquire customers.

PROCESS

CRUX Building x Restoration has clients from all walks of life. Potential visitors to the site (or clients) could be anyone from a young, tech-savvy couple to a professional housewife; from a DIYer with poor follow through to an old couple that understands the days of swinging hammers are gone. A simple site with lots of images and a simple form to contact CRUX would more than get the job done.

SOLUTION

CRUX Building x Restoration needed a website to attract and acquire customers. It needed clean, simple design and an easy to navigate UI. The two most important objectives were:
   1.  EXAMPLES images of previous jobs
   2.  CONTACT a way to get in touch
These objectives were the main inspiration for the website. Easy-to-use image galleries of job types and a contact page with a simple, short form and one CTA.
